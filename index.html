Founded with a vision to bridge creativity and justice, Dhillon Solicitors is a global online legal consultancy specialising in Entertainment Law and Workplace Compliance (POSH).


At Dhillon Solicitors, we partner with production houses, OTT platforms, artists, corporates, and startups worldwide; safeguarding intellectual property in film, music, and media while building safe, compliant workplaces. We protect what matters most: your creativity. Specializing in contracts, copyright, and IP protection, we deliver tailored legal solutions aligned with your vision and goals, ensuring artists, innovators, and businesses are protected and empowered in today’s dynamic world.


Alongside our expertise in entertainment law, Dhillon Solicitors also provides PoSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) training and consulting services for corporates, NGOs, and organisations of all sizes. Our approach goes beyond legal compliance, we focus on fostering safer, more inclusive workplaces while ensuring policies are clear, effective, and enforceable. Whether in the creative industries or the corporate sector, we are committed to protecting rights, building awareness, and creating environments where people and ideas can thrive.

PoSH Compliance & Workplace Safety

Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace

In today's workplace environment, ensuring compliance with PoSH regulations is not just a legal requirement—it's an ethical imperative. Our specialized services help organizations create safer, more inclusive work environments.


We provide comprehensive support that goes beyond basic compliance, helping you build a workplace culture where every individual feels respected, protected, and valued.


Policy Development

Custom-crafted PoSH policies aligned with latest regulations and best practices

Training & Sensitization

Specialized workshops for Internal Committees, management, and employees

Compliance Audits

Thorough assessments and reporting to ensure ongoing adherence to standards

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Our alternative dispute resolution practice offers a more harmonious path to conflict resolution, particularly valuable in the entertainment industry and workplace contexts where ongoing relationships matter.

Mediation Services

Our trained mediators facilitate constructive dialogue between parties, helping to resolve disputes without litigation. We create a neutral environment where concerns can be addressed openly and solutions can be crafted collaboratively.

Arbitration Expertise

When more formal resolution is needed, our arbitration services provide a structured yet flexible alternative to court proceedings. Our approach emphasizes efficiency, confidentiality, and expertise in entertainment and workplace matters.
